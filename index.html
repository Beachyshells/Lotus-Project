Become the Lotus

"I have seen the bottom, and now I am ready for the top" ~ unknown.

Like the lotus, you are made for transformation. No matter how deep the waters or how tangled the roots, you rise—unfolding with grace, untouched by the mud that tried to hold you. Your journey is not defined by where you began but by the brilliance of your bloom, a testament to resilience, patience, and quiet strength. Even in the most uncertain moments, trust that you are growing, reaching toward the light. You are the lotus—rooted in challenge but destined for beauty.
